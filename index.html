Day of the Death

For this October edition, I focus in one of the most important days for Mexicans, the Day of the Death.

Each year, we remember the lives of those that are not longer with us by celebrating with everything they enjoyed when alive. We eat their favorite food, we drink in their honour, and we dance at the rhythm of the songs they most enjoyed.

For the rest of the world it might be seen as something dark or evil, but to us, celebrating the Death is acknowledging that it is another chapter for everyone. It is a time to remember, to laugh, and to dance with the ones we love.

And the Quote of the day is...

A lifetime of happiness! No man alive could bear it: it would be hell on earth. George Bernard Shaw
